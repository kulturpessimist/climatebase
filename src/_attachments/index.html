Open

Our mission is to give a broad view into the jungle of carbon credits. We are open to register and show any certified project at any point in the projects lifecycle to give a deep transparent insight to every buyer of carbon credits. climatebase wants to close the loop between the creation and retierement of carbon credits. Why? Because we love our nature.

Transparent

Insted of showing only a confusingly mess of information just for retired credits we are fully commited to transparency. Only with climatebase you can see a credit at any given point in his lifecycle. We are connecting carbon buyers directly with the carbon offset projects to give the industry a more reliable surrounding. With a more open market we can enable commitment.

Sustainable

All credits are registered and tracked within climatebase via an unique serial numbers. We give you and seameless workflow from creation to the Markit Environmental Registry infrastructure. In accordance with the most premium standards, we ensures the transparency, quality, reliability and security of these carbon commodities for the marketplace.
